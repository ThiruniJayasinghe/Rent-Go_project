CAN I PAY FOR THE RENTAL OF A CAR FOR
ANOTHER INDIVIDUAL?

The renter of the vehicle must be present to sign the rental agreement and provide their credit card at the time of
pick up. The renter cannot provide a credit or debit card belonging to someone else for their use.
If you wish to pay for the rental car, but have someone else drive it, you can rent the vehicle in your name and add
them as an additional driver. In order to add an additional driver, they would have to meet all of our additional
driver requirements; those requirements can be found here.
